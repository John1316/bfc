Our Partners

Business Commercial House is the exclusive Partner of Lair for Investing AB in Jordan, a Swedish financial consulting firm that was founded by a team of professional experts, in the financial industry. Lairs founders saw a necessity in using the newest technologies in the FX market, and by being the exclusive partner of Lair in Jordan we are happy to offer our clients two new and innovative services:

Forex Recommendations

Lairs experienced team provides us with accurate, timely, and reliable recommendations to help our clients maximize their profits and minimize their losses. Accurate recommendations are generated through the use of technical analysis and the use of advanced technology such as data mining and pattern recognition algorithms. Ultimately recommendations are sent to clients through different communications channels including but not limited to email, WhatsApp, text messages, and through our client portal.

(AI) Auto Investing

Auto-Investing is the newest most rewarding way to invest in financial markets, by subscribing to our Auto-Investing packages the platform will automatically execute positions based on a predefined set of rules using a trading strategy which is based on technical analysis by Lairs professional team. By subscribing to one of the Auto-Investing packages your investment is insured by an insurance company to waive any risk that may occur through their journey with us.
